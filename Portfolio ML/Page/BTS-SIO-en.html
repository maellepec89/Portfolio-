What is BTS SIO ?

BTS SIO (Computer Service to Organization) is a 2-year training course that prepares for the digital profession. It can take place in initial training or in apprenticeship.

This training has 2 options :

- SLAM option : Software Solutions and Business Applications
. Support and provision of IT services
. Analysis, design and development of applications
. Development of WEB sites
. Cybersecurity of IT services

- SISR option : Infrastructure, Systems and Networking Solutions
. Network infrastructure design
. System administration (Windows, Linux, ...)
. Network supervision
. Security, cybersecurity

The general education subjects are as follows :

- General culture and expression
- English language
- Economic, managerial and legal culture for IT
- Mathematics for computer science
- Optional : Advanced mathematics
